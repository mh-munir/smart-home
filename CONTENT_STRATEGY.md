## 💡 SEO Best Practices

### Title Optimization:
```
Good: "Best Smart Door Locks 2026: Expert Reviews & Buying Guide"
Better: "10 Best Smart Door Locks for Home Security (2026 Reviews)"
Best: "Smart Door Locks 2026: Top 5 Picks & Complete Buyer's Guide"
```

### Keyword Research Tools:
- Google Search Console (free) - see your keywords
- Google Keyword Planner (free)
- Ubersuggest (free tier)
- Ahrefs (paid)

### Internal Linking Strategy:
```
Homepage
  ↓
Pillar Blog (Smart Home Setup)
  ├→ Specific Blog (Best Door Locks)
  │   └→ Product Page
  ├→ Specific Blog (Best Cameras)
  │   └→ Product Page
  └→ Specific Blog (Best Lights)
      └→ Product Page
```

### External Linking:
- Link to authority sites: Amazon.com, CNET, Wirecutter
- Adds credibility to your content
- Helps with SEO

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't:**
- Copy content from other sites
- Add too many affiliate links (annoying)
- Ignore analytics
- Forget to optimize meta tags
- Use bad/broken images
- Target super competitive keywords first
- Spam social media

✅ **Do:**
- Write original content
- 1-3 relevant affiliate links per post
- Check metrics weekly
- Always optimize titles/descriptions
- Use high-quality images
- Start with long-tail keywords
- Be helpful and genuine
