❌ **Don't**: Keyword stuff ("home smart products products products")
✅ **Do**: Use naturally ("home smart products for your bedroom")

❌ **Don't**: Copy competitor content exactly
✅ **Do**: Write original, unique perspectives

❌ **Don't**: Hide text or links (white text on white background)
✅ **Do**: Make everything visible and accessible

❌ **Don't**: Buy low-quality backlinks

❌ **Don't**: Ignore Google Search Console warnings
✅ **Do**: Fix errors promptly

❌ **Don't**: Cloak content (show different content to Google)
✅ **Do**: Show same content to all users
